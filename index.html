Selected Use Cases

What this looks like in practice.

A few real examples of the work — and the results it delivered.

Onboarding · Fintech
Fixing a leaky verification funnel
New customers were giving up partway through sign-up, and no one could see where. We found the sticking points and tested fixes properly to prove they worked.
ImpactA measurable lift in new accounts — roughly 11K additional accounts a year — and a repeatable way to keep improving onboarding.
Reporting · Retail
Retiring a wall of manual spreadsheets
Weekly reporting depended on dozens of hand-updated spreadsheets — slow and easy to get wrong. We replaced them with reports that update automatically.
ImpactReporting time cut by roughly 90%, with far fewer errors and insight available the moment leaders needed it.
Forecasting · Consumer Goods
Closing the demand–supply gap
A seasonal product was always either over- or under-stocked. We built a way to predict demand and plan deliveries more cleverly.
ImpactA tighter demand–supply match, lower transportation cost, and healthier margins through the peak season.
Risk · Financial Services
Seeing fraud before it scaled
Warning signs were scattered everywhere with no clear view. We pulled them together so suspicious activity showed up early, in one place.
ImpactProactive monitoring that meaningfully reduced multi-million-dollar fraud losses and informed executive strategy.
Experimentation · Retail Media
Making product placement a decision, not a guess
Decisions about what to put where relied on gut feel. We found which products sell together and tested better placements.
ImpactEvidence-based placement decisions that lifted in-store sales and gave teams a model they could reuse.
Pricing · Banking
Bringing clarity to pricing and margin
Pricing decisions were being made without a clear sense of what actually made money. We built the picture that showed where the profit really was.
ImpactLeadership gained a defensible view of profitability — and a clearer line to growth opportunities.
